Art Gallery

Starry Night

Vincent van Gogh

1889

A masterpiece of Post-Impressionism, depicting the view from the window of his room at the asylum of Saint-Rémy-de-Provence.

Las Meninas

Diego Velázquez

1656

A masterpiece of the Spanish Baroque, depicting a complex and enigmatic scene from the royal court of King Philip IV of Spain. The painting offers a fascinating glimpse into the artist's studio and the interaction between the subjects and the viewer.

The Scream

Edvard Munch

1893

A quintessential work of Expressionism, depicting an anguished figure against a tumultuous sky. The painting captures a moment of existential dread and emotional intensity.

Les Demoiselles d'Avignon

Pablo Picasso

1907

A groundbreaking work of Cubism, depicting five female figures in a fragmented and abstract style. The painting challenges traditional representations of the female form and is considered a precursor to modern art.D

Café Terrace at Night

Vincent van Gogh

1888

An evocative work of Post-Impressionism, showing a nighttime view of a café in Arles. The painting is renowned for its vibrant colors and starry sky, capturing the lively atmosphere of the terrace.

The Birth of Venus

Sandro Botticelli

c. 1484–1486

A renowned masterpiece of the Italian Renaissance, depicting the goddess Venus emerging from the sea on a shell. The painting is celebrated for its beauty and its representation of classical mythology.
